How it Works

From inquiry to gathering.

Ordering is simple — here's what to expect.

Easy as brie, 1, 2, 3.
  1. 1

    Reach out

    Fill out the order form with your date, size, and occasion.

  2. 2

    We plan it

    We confirm the details, dietary needs, and a 50% deposit to book.

  3. 3

    Handcrafted fresh

    Your board is built by hand the day of, for peak freshness.

  4. 4

    Gather & enjoy

    Pickup or local delivery — then it's time to graze.
