Our Story

A little cheese, a lot of love.

Handcrafted, made fresh, and built to bring people together.

This isn't just a hobby — it's our brie and butter.

Cheese and Thingz started in 2020 as a labor of love — a way to bring people together around a beautiful table. Every board is arranged by hand with carefully sourced cheeses, cured meats, fresh and dried fruit, nuts, honey, and the little "thingz" that make each one special.

Whether it's a cozy date night, a bridal shower, a game-day spread, or a grazing table for your next big celebration, we build it fresh and make it unforgettable.

  • Handcrafted & made fresh to order
  • Locally sourced when we can
  • Customized for your occasion & dietary needs
  • Pickup or local delivery
