FAQ

Good questions, grate answers.

Everything you need to know before you order.

No question is too cheesy.
How much notice do you need?

We ask for at least 48–72 hours for standard boards, and 1–2 weeks for grazing tables and large events. Need something sooner? Reach out anyway — we'll do our best to make it work.

Do you deliver, or is it pickup only?

Both! Pickup is free in East Dundee. We also offer local delivery within the surrounding NW Chicago suburbs for a small fee based on distance. We'll confirm the delivery cost when you order.

Can you handle allergies and dietary needs?

Absolutely. Just let us know in your order — we can build vegetarian, gluten-conscious, nut-free, and pork-free boards. Please note boards are made in a home kitchen that also handles common allergens.

How do I pay, and is a deposit required?

We take a 50% deposit to lock in your date, with the balance due at pickup or delivery. We accept Venmo, Zelle, and cash. Your date isn't reserved until the deposit is received.

What's your cancellation policy?

Life happens! Cancel 72+ hours ahead for a full deposit refund. Within 72 hours, the deposit is non-refundable since we've already sourced your ingredients — but we're happy to reschedule when we can.

How long do the boards keep?

Boards are built fresh the day of your event. For the best taste and texture, enjoy within a few hours of pickup/delivery, and refrigerate anything you're saving for later.

Can I customize my board?

Yes — every board is customized! Tell us your occasion, favorites, and any must-haves (or must-avoids) and we'll tailor it. Add-ons like dips, chocolate-dipped fruit, honey, and florals are always available.

Still have a question? We'd love to help.
